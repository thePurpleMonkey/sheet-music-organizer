Setlists
Setlists are a group of songs that can be shared with others. You can add any songs from your collection and put them in any order. By default, only you can see setlists that you create. You can share setlists with other members of the collection, or you can share them publicly with a link – no login required. After creating a setlist, go to Options -> Share and choose who to share the setlist with.
